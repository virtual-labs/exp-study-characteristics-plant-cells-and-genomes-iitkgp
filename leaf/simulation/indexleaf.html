Instructions
  1. Click on the blade to take a small section of leaf area between veins.
  2. Click on the small section of the leaf to place it in a cork.
  3. Click on the blade to cut a small section of the leaf.
  4. The leaf section is shifted section in watch glass / petridish containing water with the help of a brush or blade.
  5. Click on the leaf section in the petridish to transfer onto a glass microscope slide.
  6. Click on the dropper to draw water from the beaker. Click to move the dropper above glass slide. Click to add a drop of water to the leaf.
  7. Click on the coverslip to place over the leaf section
  8. Click on the prepared slide to place on the stage of the microscope.
  9. Start with the magnification objective lens. You will see a figure like this of the cross section of the leaf
Equipement required
